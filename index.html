About Cousins & Co.

We are more than just photographers; we are storytellers. Founded on a shared passion for capturing life's fleeting moments, Cousins & Co. brings a personal and artistic touch to every project. Our philosophy is simple: create beautiful, timeless images that you and your loved ones will cherish for generations.

With a blend of classic technique and modern creativity, we specialize in making you feel comfortable in front of the lens, resulting in authentic and emotional photographs.
